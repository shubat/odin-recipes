Turkey Sandwich

Everyone loves a turkey sandwich anytime of the day.

Here is my secret recipe for the Turkey Sandwich:

  1. Ingredients
    • Toast
    • butter
    • Turkey Breast
    • Swiss Cheese
  2. Steps
    1. spread the butter on the toast
    2. grill the toast on low heat for two mintues
    3. add the turkey and cheese
    4. close the sandwich
    5. regrill it for one minute to melt the chees
