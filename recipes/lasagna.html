Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is one of the world most popular dishes that all members of a family likes from toddlers to grandpas.

Here is my secret recipe for the lasagna:

  1. Ingredients
    • Meat
    • Onion and garlic
    • Tomato
    • Lasagna Pasta
    • mozzarella
  2. Steps
    1. Make the meat sauce.
    2. Cook the noodles.
    3. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
    4. Cover with foil and bake.
    5. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
    6. Put Mozzarella on top
